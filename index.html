How to use v0.5

Choose a scene, camera mode and lens. Adjust ISO, aperture and shutter speed using the dials.

The exposure meter reacts immediately. Press the shutter to receive a score and instructor feedback.

Keyboard: I changes ISO, A changes aperture, S changes shutter speed, and Space takes a photo.
